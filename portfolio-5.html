Booking hotels in South Africa have never been easier.

This website was built for the guests and customers of The Capital hotels that are scattered through several major cities of South Africa - Sandton, Cape Town, Rosebank, etc.

Polished workflow with both desktop and mobile responsive UIs helps people to place their booking in no time.

I have built this website using React.js, and its CMS using Google Firebase.
